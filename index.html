What is Aura OS?

Interactive Resume

Why read a boring PDF when you can explore skills, projects, and experiences through interactive applications like a File Explorer and Terminal?

Desktop Grade Feel

Designed to feel exactly like a modern operating system with a dock, control center, dynamic wallpapers, window management, and native system sounds.

React & Framer Motion

Built with cutting edge web technologies to ensure buttery smooth 60fps animations, seamless transitions, and immediate interaction response.

Fluid Window Management

Open multiple apps concurrently. Drag, minimize, maximize, and arrange your workspace exactly how you want it. It's multi-tasking built for the browser.

Dynamic Control Center

Toggle Dark Mode, alter system brightness, change the aesthetic wallpaper, or silence notifications via a beautifully frosted control center that persists your preferences.

Install on iOS

1

Open aura-os-theta.vercel.app on your iPhone's Safari browser.

2

Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen (the square with an arrow pointing up).

3

Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".

This will install Aura OS directly on your device just like a native app!
